Car Detection


Project for Samsung Innovation Campus:
Hidekel Araujo Pinales



Objective of the project
This project aims to offer vehicle traffic analysis services to local businesses, 
helping them understand traffic patterns and optimize their marketing strategies 
based on the data collected. In addition to this in the area of 
Digital Advertising: Implementing car detection systems that integrate with digital screens 
to show personalized ads based on the type of vehicle and other detected data.
